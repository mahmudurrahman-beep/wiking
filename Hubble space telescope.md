## Key Facts
- **Launch Date:** April 24, 1990 (Space Shuttle Discovery, STS-31).  
- **Orbit:** Low Earth orbit, ~540 km altitude.  
- **Size:** 13.2 m long, 4.2 m wide.  
- **Power:** 2,800 watts from solar panels.  
- **Operator:** NASA & ESA, managed by STScI.  

## Major Discoveries
- Determined the **age of the universe (~13.8 billion years)**.  
- Provided evidence for **dark energy**, explaining accelerated expansion.  
- Captured iconic images like the **Pillars of Creation** in the Eagle Nebula.  
- Studied atmospheres of exoplanets.  
- Revealed galaxies forming in the early universe.
