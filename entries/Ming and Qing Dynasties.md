# Ming and Qing Dynasties

## Ming Dynasty (1368–1644)
- Founded by **Zhu Yuanzhang (Hongwu Emperor)**; restored Han Chinese rule after the Yuan.  
- Achievements: Forbidden City, Zheng He’s voyages, flourishing arts and porcelain.  
- Decline due to internal strife and external pressures; overthrown in 1644.

## Qing Dynasty (1644–1912)
- Established by the **Manchus**; expanded China’s territory and presided over a cultural high point in the 18th century.  
- Decline from internal rebellions, corruption, and foreign encroachment; ended with the 1911 revolution and the founding of the Republic of China.
