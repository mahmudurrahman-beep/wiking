## Early Life
- Born in **New Delhi, India**.  
- Studied at **Hansraj College (BA in Economics)** and later at Jamia Millia Islamia (Mass Communication).  
- Began his career in television with shows like *Fauji* (1989).  

## Film Career
- Debut: *Deewana* (1992).  
- Known for iconic films:  
  - *Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (1995)*  
  - *Dil Se (1998)*  
  - *My Name is Khan (2010)*  
  - *Chennai Express (2013)*  
  - *Pathaan (2023)* and *Jawan (2023)*.  
- Nicknamed **“King Khan”** for his dominance in Bollywood.  

## Achievements
- Over **100 films** across genres.  
- Awards: **Padma Shri (2005)**, **Legion of Honour (2014)**, and multiple Filmfare Awards.  
- Founder of **Red Chillies Entertainment** and co-owner of **Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR)** IPL team.
