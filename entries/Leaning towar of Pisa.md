## Location
- Pisa, Tuscany, Italy.  
- Part of the cathedral complex known as the **Piazza dei Miracoli (Square of Miracles)**.  

## Construction
- Began in **1173**, completed in the 14th century.  
- Built as the freestanding **bell tower** for Pisaâ€™s cathedral.  
- Height: about **56 meters (183 ft)**.  

## The Lean
- The tower began leaning during construction due to **unstable, soft ground** (clay, sand, and shells).  
- Tilt reached about **5.5 degrees** before stabilization efforts.  
- Modern engineering reduced the lean to about **3.97 degrees**, ensuring safety.  

## Significance
- Iconic symbol of Italy and medieval architecture.  
- UNESCO World Heritage Site.  
- Attracts millions of tourists every year.
