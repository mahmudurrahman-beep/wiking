## Introduction
Jupiter is the fifth planet from the Sun and the **largest in the Solar System**. Classified as a gas giant, it is composed mainly of hydrogen and helium. Its immense size and powerful magnetic field make it one of the most influential planets in shaping the Solar System.

## History / Origins
- Known since antiquity; named after **Jupiter**, the king of the Roman gods.  
- Observed by Galileo in 1610, who discovered its four largest moons (the Galilean moons).  
- Studied extensively by spacecraft such as **Pioneer**, **Voyager**, **Galileo**, and **Juno**.
