## Introduction

## History / Origins
- Established as early as the **4th century BCE** by the Nabataeans, a nomadic Arab people.  
- Became a major trading hub linking Arabia, Egypt, and the Mediterranean.  
- Conquered by the Romans in **106 CE**, who added new structures like temples and colonnaded streets.  
- Declined after trade routes shifted and earthquakes damaged the city.  
- Rediscovered for the outside world in **1812** by Swiss explorer **Johann Ludwig Burckhardt**.
