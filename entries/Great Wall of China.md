## Introduction
The **Great Wall of China** is a series of fortifications built across northern China to protect against invasions. Stretching thousands of kilometers, it is one of the most iconic monuments in world history and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

- **Length**: Over 21,000 kilometers (13,000 miles) including all branches.  
- **Structure**: Made of stone, brick, tamped earth, and wood depending on region.  
- **Watchtowers**: Used for signaling and defense.
