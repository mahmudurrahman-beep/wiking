# Space Exploration

The exploration of outer space using robotic probes, satellites, and human missions.

## Early Milestones
- **1957:** Sputnik 1, first artificial satellite.  
- **1961:** First human in space (Yuri Gagarin).  
- **1969:** Apollo 11 Moon landing.

## Expansion and Tools
- Robotic probes (Voyager, Mars rovers), space telescopes (Hubble), and space stations (Mir, ISS).  
- Space shuttle era enabled reusable orbital operations.

## Modern Era and Future
- Commercial spaceflight, reusable launch systems, plans for Mars missions, asteroid exploration, and deep-space science.
