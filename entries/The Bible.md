# The Bible

The **Bible** is the central sacred text of **Christianity**, divided into the **Old Testament** and the **New Testament**.

## Structure
- **Old Testament:** Law, history, poetry, and prophecy (shared in part with Judaism).  
- **New Testament:** Life and teachings of **Jesus Christ**, the Gospels, Acts, Epistles, and Revelation.

## Themes
- Covenant, salvation, moral teaching, and the life and message of Jesus.

## Significance
- Foundation for Christian belief, worship, ethics, and cultural influence across the world.
