## Introduction
**Boeing** is an American multinational corporation that designs, manufactures, and sells airplanes, rotorcraft, rockets, satellites, telecommunications equipment, and missiles worldwide. It is one of the largest aerospace companies and defense contractors in the world.

## History / Origins
- Founded on **July 15, 1916** in Seattle, Washington by **William E. Boeing**.  
- Originally called **Pacific Aero Products Co.**, renamed Boeing Airplane Company in 1917.  
- Played a major role in aviation history, producing military aircraft during World War II and pioneering commercial jetliners.  
- Moved headquarters to **Chicago, Illinois** in 2001, and later to **Arlington, Virginia** in 2022.
