## Key Facts
- Location: **Dubai, UAE**.  
- Height: **828 m (2,717 ft)**.  
- Floors: **163**.  
- Construction: Began in **2004**, completed in **2009**, opened **2010**.  
- Cost: Approx. **US$1.5 billion**.  
- Developer: **Emaar Properties**.  
- Architect: **Adrian Smith (SOM)**.  

## Design & Architecture
- Style: **Neo-futurism**.  
- Inspired by Islamic architecture (spiraling minaret design).  
- Structural system: reinforced concrete and steel.  
- Exterior: glass, aluminum, and steel panels.  

## Features
- **Observation decks:**  
  - Level 124 & 125 (*At the Top*).  
  - Level 148 (*At the Top SKY*), one of the highest observation decks in the world.  
- **Residential apartments, offices, hotels, restaurants, and lounges**.  
- Houses the **Armani Hotel Dubai**.  

## Records
- Tallest building in the world since **2009**.  
- Tallest free-standing structure.  
- Highest occupied floor.  
- Longest elevator travel distance.
