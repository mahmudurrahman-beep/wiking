## Origins
- First released: **November 15, 2001** in North America.  
- Microsoft’s entry into the gaming console market.  
- Introduced **Xbox Live**, an online gaming service that revolutionized multiplayer gaming.  

## Console Generations
- **Xbox (2001)** – Original console, introduced Halo: Combat Evolved.  
- **Xbox 360 (2005)** – Huge success, sold ~84 million units; introduced achievements and Xbox Live Marketplace.  
- **Xbox One (2013)** – Focused on multimedia integration, Kinect, and digital services.  
- **Xbox Series X|S (2020)** – Current generation; Series X is high‑performance, Series S is affordable digital‑only.  

## Services
- **Xbox Live** – Online multiplayer, achievements, digital store.  
- **Xbox Game Pass** – Subscription service offering hundreds of games, including day‑one releases.  
- **Xbox Cloud Gaming (xCloud)** – Play games via streaming on mobile, PC, and consoles.  

## Cultural Impact
- Iconic franchises: **Halo, Gears of War, Forza, Fable**.  
- Pioneered online console gaming with Xbox Live.  
- Strong focus on **backward compatibility** and cross‑platform play.  

## Legacy
Xbox is not just a console but a **gaming ecosystem**, combining hardware, software, and cloud services. It remains a major player in the global gaming industry alongside PlayStation and Nintendo.
