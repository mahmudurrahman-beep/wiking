Elon Musk (born June 28, 1971) is a South African‑born American entrepreneur, engineer, and inventor. He is best known as the founder, CEO, and chief engineer of **SpaceX**, CEO and product architect of **Tesla, Inc.**, and owner of **X (formerly Twitter)**. Musk is recognized for his ambitious vision of advancing space exploration, sustainable energy, and artificial intelligence.

## Early Life

- Born in Pretoria, South Africa.  
- Developed an early interest in computing and technology.  
- Moved to the United States via Canada in the 1990s.  
- Studied at Queen’s University (Canada) and later transferred to the University of Pennsylvania.  

## Career Milestones

1. **Zip2 (1996)**  
   - Co‑founded with his brother Kimbal Musk.  
   - Provided online city guide software for newspapers.  
   - Sold to Compaq in 1999.  

2. **X.com and PayPal (1999–2002)**  
   - Founded X.com, an online payments company.  
   - Later became PayPal, acquired by eBay in 2002.  

3. **SpaceX (2002–present)**  
   - Founded to reduce space transportation costs and enable Mars colonization.  
   - Developed Falcon rockets and Dragon spacecraft.  
   - Achieved reusable rocket technology.  
   - Partnered with NASA for crewed missions to the ISS.  

4. **Tesla, Inc. (2004–present)**  
   - Joined as chairman and later CEO.  
   - Revolutionized electric vehicles with the Roadster, Model S, Model 3, and Model Y.  
   - Expanded into solar energy and battery storage.  

5. **Other Ventures**  
   - **Neuralink** – Brain‑computer interface research.  
   - **The Boring Company** – Tunnel construction and urban transport solutions.  
   - **OpenAI** – Co‑founder, focused on artificial intelligence research.  
   - **X (formerly Twitter)** – Acquired in 2022, rebranded as X in 2023.  

## Personal Life

- Holds citizenship in South Africa, Canada, and the United States.  
- Has multiple children.  
- Known for outspoken presence on social media.  

## Wealth and Recognition

- Frequently ranked among the world’s richest individuals.  
- Net worth has fluctuated between $200–300 billion in recent years.  
- Named Time’s *Person of the Year* in 2021.  

## Legacy and Impact

* Pioneered reusable rockets, making spaceflight more affordable.  
* Accelerated global adoption of electric vehicles.  
* Advocated for sustainable energy and climate solutions.  
* Invested in AI and neurotechnology with long‑term visions of human advancement.
