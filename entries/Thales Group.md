## Introduction
**Thales Group** is a global technology leader in aerospace, defense, transportation, and cybersecurity. The company provides solutions to governments, institutions, and businesses, helping them carry out critical missions with advanced technology.

## History / Origins
- Founded in **2000** as **Thales S.A.**, evolving from **Thomson-CSF**, a French electronics and defense company.  
- Expanded through acquisitions, including **Gemalto** (digital identity and security) in 2019.  
- Today, Thales is a major player in both defense and civilian technology markets.
