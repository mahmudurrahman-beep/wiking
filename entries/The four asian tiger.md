## Why They’re Called “Tigers”
- Achieved **extraordinary economic growth** from the 1960s onward.  
- Transitioned from low-income economies to advanced, high-income ones.  
- Known for **export-driven industrialization**, strong education systems, and government-led development policies.  

## Singapore’s Role
- Leveraged its strategic location as a global port.  
- Built a strong financial sector and high-tech industries.  
- Today, one of the world’s richest countries by GDP per capita.
